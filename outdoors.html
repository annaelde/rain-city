Outdoors

One of the most beautiful features of Seattle is the wilderness surrounding it. According to Martucci, visiting the wilderness around Seattle is one of the top activities in the area. In the city itself, there are numerous parks, such as Ravenna Park, encompassing a ravine in the University District, and Discovery Park, towards the shores of the Puget Sound.

But if you journey outside the city's limits, you'll find even more gorgeous wilderness to experience. Below is an image gallery depicting the nearby Olympic Peninsula, only a couple of hours away from Seattle by car.
